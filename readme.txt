== Description ==

Add your custom logo at login screen with one simple step.

== Installation ==

1.Upload the  plugin to your blog and just activate it.
2.Goto your activated theme's images folder (E.g. yourdomain.com/wp-content/themes/yourtheme/images) and put your logo of dimensions about 325* 85 and the name of that logo must be login_logo.png.

That's all! I hope you enjoy the plugin.
